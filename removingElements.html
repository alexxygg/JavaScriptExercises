Removing Elements

removeChild

This will remove the child of the parent element.

We can do it in 2 ways:

Either declare a variable for the parent and for the child elements...

Or call the parentElement method on the declared child element and pass in the childElement into the parenthesis.

There were 3 list items as in the second unordered list, but after removing 2 we were left with this.

remove

This will remove the element directly!
