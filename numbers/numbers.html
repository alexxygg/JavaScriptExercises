Numbers

They are a primitive type of value in JS.

There are decimal numbers, integers, fractions, negatives, etc.

We can add, subtract, multiply, divide, get remainders, etc.

2, -2, 2.568

1+2, 2-2, 3*7, 8/3, 8%2.

Power of **

The power of symbol helps us multiple a number to the power of another number.

7 ** 3 is the same as 7 * 7 * 7

8 ** 4 is the same as 8 * 8 * 8 * 8

Operations (PEMDAS)

When multiple operations are expected, it will go by importance, which is parenthesis, exponents, multiplication, division, addition and subtraction.

This: 4+5*6 is the same as 4+(5*6)

4 + 3 * 4 / 2 is the same as 4 + ((3 * 4)/2) = 10

(13 % 5) ** 2 is equal to (3) ** 2 = 9.

Remainders

The percentage symbol helps us do operations as remainders. They help to determine if a number is even or odd in some ways.

All these result in 1 :

25489%2, 323%2, 45677%2

The numbers can be divided by 2 and have 1 left over.

All these result in 0 :

25488%2, 324%2, 45678%2

The numbers can be divided by 2 exactly.

NaN

Not a Number:

0 divided by zero is an example.

1 + NaN is also NaN.

200 + 0/0 is also NaN.

We can not do any math with NaN, anything you multiple, add to a NaN will result in NaN.

typeof

typeof helps us know what something is.

typeof NaN = number
