Nested Loops

We cna nest a loop inside of another loop.

First, our original loop will run once.

Then, our nested loop will run UNTIL THE EXPRESSION IS FALSE.

We will end up with an output for our original loop, and ans many outputs for our second loop as long as they are TRUE. This will happen every time until our ORIGINAL LOOP IS FALSE.
